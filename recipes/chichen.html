Chicken and Vegetables

This delicious slow cooker meal of chicken and vegetables pairs up nicely with couscous and pita bread.

by DEBMCE4

Temps de preparation

  • Prep : 20 mins
  • Cook : 6 h 30 mins
  • Total : 6 h 50 mins
  • Serving : 4
  • Yield : 4 Serving

ingredients

  1. 12 pepperoncini peppers, rinsed and drained
  2. 1 cup sliced pitted kalamata olives
  3. 8 cloves minced garlic
  4. 3 ½ pounds chicken leg quarters
  5. 1 ½ teaspoons paprika
  6. ¼ teaspoon salt
  7. ¼ teaspoon fresh ground pepper
  8. ½ teaspoon grated lemon zest
  9. ½ cup fresh-squeezed lemon juice
  10. 1 cup sour cream
  11. ½ teaspoon paprika

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Layer whole pepperoncini on the bottom of a slow cooker. Sprinkle the olive slices and garlic on top of the peppers.

  2. Step 2

    3. Rinse chicken and pat dry. Place on top of pepperoncini mixture. Sprinkle chicken with 1 1/2 teaspoons paprika, the salt, pepper and lemon zest. Slowly pour in lemon juice.

  3. Step 3

    4. Cover and cook on low 6 to 6 1/2 hours or until meat easily pulls away from bone. Remove chicken to a warm plate and cover to keep warm.

  4. Step 4

    5. Turn slow cooker on high. Skim fat from cooking liquid. Whisk in sour cream until blended. Cover and simmer on high until heated through 8 to 10 minutes depending on your cooker. Stir in pepper and paprika.
